# CCM Module – PPT Slide Content (Copy-Paste Ready)

---

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**Title:** Class Committee Meeting (CCM) Module
**Subtitle:** Structured Academic Governance for Classes
**Supporting Line:** Transparent. Accountable. Action-Oriented.

---

## Slide 2: What is a CCM?
**Header:** Class Committee Meeting Overview
**Bullet Points:**
- A formal interface between Faculty (Class Advisor) and Student Representatives.
- Held periodically to discuss academic progress, infrastructure, and feedback.
- All discussions and decisions are digitally documented for HOD review.
- Ensures issues raised by students are actioned with accountability.

---

## Slide 3: Who is Involved?
**Header:** Roles & Responsibilities
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Class Advisor (Staff) | Initiates meeting, records minutes, assigns action items |
| Student Representative | Voices class concerns, receives meeting notification |
| HOD | Views all CCM records for departmental oversight |

---

## Slide 4: Meeting Lifecycle
**Header:** Step-by-Step CCM Workflow
**Bullet Points:**
1. **Create:** Advisor selects date, category, and agenda.
2. **Invite:** Select student representatives — they receive an automatic notification.
3. **Record:** Notes, decisions, and attendance are logged.
4. **Assign:** Action items set with a responsible person and deadline.
5. **Track:** Each action item moves from Pending → Completed.
6. **Review:** HOD monitors all meetings across the department.

---

## Slide 5: CCM Categories
**Header:** Four Types of Meetings
**Bullet Points:**
- 📚 **Academic** – Syllabus coverage, assignment workload, learning issues.
- 🏫 **Infrastructure** – Lab equipment, classroom facilities, internet access.
- 👩‍🏫 **Faculty** – Teaching methodology, availability, communication.
- 📝 **Exam** – Exam schedule, internal mark concerns, evaluation transparency.

---

## Slide 6: Key Features
**Header:** What Makes this Module Powerful
**Bullet Points:**
- **Action Item Tracker:** Each task has a responsible person, deadline, and status.
- **Minutes PDF Upload:** Official documents can be attached to the meeting record.
- **Role-Based Visibility:** Staff sees only their class; HOD sees the full department.
- **Audit Logs:** Every creation is logged for governance and transparency.
- **Real-Time Notifications:** Student reps instantly notified upon selection.

---

## Slide 7: Data Captured in Each CCM
**Header:** Information Stored per Meeting
**Bullet Points:**
- Meeting date, category, and academic year.
- Agenda, detailed notes, and final decisions.
- Student representative names and attendance count.
- Action items with person responsible and status.
- Optional attached PDF of formal minutes.

---

## Slide 8: CCM Impact
**Header:** Benefits to the Institution
**Bullet Points:**
- Promotes student voice in academic governance.
- Reduces unresolved grievances through trackable action items.
- Enables HOD to spot department-wide patterns and issues.
- Creates a verifiable audit trail of all class-level decisions.
