Slide 2: Abstract (The "Big Picture")
• The Problem: Traditional academic systems are fragmented. Manual grading is slow, mentorship is reactive (not proactive), and institutional leaders lack real-time "risk intelligence."
• The Solution: AAES is a unified engine that automates the "busy work" (grading) while providing high-fidelity data to humans for "meaningful work" (mentoring). 
• Key Methodology: Combines Python-based AI Evaluation (OCR + LLMs) with an Executive Governance Suite. 
• Impact: Reduces evaluation turnaround from days to seconds and identifies at-risk students weeks before they fail.

Slide 3: Comprehensive User Roles
• 1. Students: Submit theory/code; get instant AI feedback; track academic growth.
• 2. Staff (Faculty): Course management; hybrid AI-Human grading; attendance & CCM tracking.
• 3. Mentors (Advisors): Proactive monitoring of specific student groups; counseling logs; performance alerts.
• 4. HODs: Department-level audit; faculty performance analytics; curriculum oversight.
• 5. Principal (Executive): Institutional risk intelligence; infrastructure auditing; policy settings.
• 6. Admin: Core system config; bulk data management; semester lifecycle transitions.

Slide 4: Feature Pillar 1 - Hybrid AI Evaluation Engine
• Theory Assessment: Extracts text from PDFs or handwritten images via OCR; compares concepts against model answers using Local LLMs.
• Programming Assessment: Executes code in a secure sandboxed environment; validates against hidden test cases with CPU/Memory limits.
• Human-in-the-loop: AI provides "Draft Marks" and "Feedback"; Staff have final locking/overriding authority to ensure academic integrity.

Slide 5: Feature Pillar 2 - Mentorship & 360° Tracking
• Cross-Subject View: Unlike typical portals, a Mentor sees a student's marks in ALL subjects, enabling holistic intervention.
• Behavioral Logging: Digital tracking of 1-on-1 counseling, disciplinary actions, and family communication.
• Early Warning System: Automated alerts if a student’s attendance or marks drop below a customizable threshold.

Slide 6: Feature Pillar 3 - Governance & Risk Intelligence
• Risk Dashboard: A data-driven view for HODs/Principals categorizing students as "Healthy," "At Risk," or "Critical" using real-time algorithms.
• Class Committee Meetings (CCM): Fully digital workflow for recording student grievances and tracking "Action Taken" reports.
• Institutional Audit: Principals can see real-time staff engagement, subject completion rates, and system adoption metrics.

Slide 7: Example Workflow A - The Evaluator Path
1. Staff: Uploads a "Model Answer" and starts a theory assignment.
2. Student: Uploads a photo of their handwritten paper.
3. System (OCR + AI): Transcribes text -> Analyzes content -> Assigns 7/10 with feedback on "missing technical keywords."
4. Staff: Reviews the AI suggestion, adds 1 mark for neatness, and "Publishes" the result.
5. Notification: Student receives result; Mentor is notified if this score causes a performance dip.

Slide 8: Example Workflow B - The Governance Path
1. System: Detects Student X has missed 3 consecutive classes and failed a quiz.
2. Alert: The "Risk Intelligence" engine flags Student X as "At-Risk" on the Mentor's dashboard.
3. Intervention: Mentor schedules a 1-on-1, logs the session in AAES, and notifies the HOD.
4. Resolution: Student improves; high-level analytics show the "intervention success rate" to the Principal.

Slide 9: Technical Architecture
• Modern Frontend: React.js with Tailwind CSS for a premium "Glassmorphism" UI.
• Robust Backend: Node.js/Express with MongoDB for distributed academic data.
• Intelligence Layer: Python FastAPI service managing OCR (Tesseract) and LLM (Ollama/Local AI) to ensure data privacy.
• Security: Sandbox code execution prevents malicious student scripts from affecting the server.

Slide 10: Conclusion & Future Vision
• The "AAES" Value: It's not just a tool; it's a digital transformation of institutional culture.
• Effectiveness: 90% reduction in grading time; 100% visibility into student risk factors.
• Future scope: Predictive AI for placement success and integration with regional university data silos.
