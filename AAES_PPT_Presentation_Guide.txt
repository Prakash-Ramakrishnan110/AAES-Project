AAES - Automated Assignment Evaluation System - Presentation Content (Detailed)

Slide 1: Title Slide
• Project Title: AAES - Automated Academic Evaluation System
• A comprehensive, AI-powered platform to automate academic evaluation, mentorship, and institutional governance.
• Presented by: [Your Name/Team]

Slide 2: Abstract
• Holistic Ecosystem: AAES is not just an assignment grader; it's a complete academic lifecycle manager.
• Core Pillars: 
  1. AI-Powered Evaluation (Theory OCR & Python Code)
  2. Dedicated Mentorship (Mentor-Mentee tracking)
  3. Class Committee Meetings (CCM for student feedback)
  4. Institutional Governance (Principal/HOD Risk Analytics)
• Unified Roles: Dedicated, customized dashboards for Students, Staff, Mentors, HODs, Administration, and Principals.

Slide 3: Problem Statement
• Evaluation Bottlenecks: Faculty spend excessive time on repetitive manual grading, leading to delayed feedback for students.
• Fragmented Mentorship: Advisors lack centralized data to track a student's holistic performance across all subjects.
• Poor Feedback Loops: Traditional Class Committee Meetings (CCM) lack digital tracking and actionable follow-ups.
• Blind Spots in Governance: Principals and HODs lack real-time visibility into "At-Risk" students until end-of-semester.

Slide 4: Proposed System (AAES) Solution
• AI-Driven Grading: Uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR), LLMs, and Safe Code Execution for instant, accurate marking.
• Centralized Mentorship: Unified advisor portals providing a 360-degree view of a mentee's attendance, grades, and behavioral notes.
• Digital CCM Portal: Systematic recording of student grievances, meeting minutes, and action items.
• Real-time Risk Analytics: Automated flagging of students struggling academically or lacking attendance.

Slide 5: Key Feature 1 - Intelligent Assignment Evaluation
• Easy Creation: Staff configure theory essays, quizzes, or programming tasks with custom test cases.
• Automated Processing: 
   - Theory: Image -> OCR -> AI Comparison against Model Answer -> Score & Feedback.
   - Programming: Secure Sandbox Execution -> Automated Test Cases -> Score.
• Integrity Tools: Built-in Plagiarism Detection for all document submissions.
• Resubmission Control: Built-in workflows for students to request re-evaluations for low scores.

Slide 6: Key Feature 2 - Mentorship & Student Tracking (Mentor/Mentee)
• Mentor Dashboard: Advisors are assigned specific mentees to monitor continuously.
• 360-Degree View: Mentors can see their mentees' performance across EVERY subject they are enrolled in, not just the mentor's own class.
• Advisor Notes: Secure digital logging of behavioral issues, academic progress, and 1-on-1 counseling sessions.
• Early Intervention: Mentors are instantly alerted to failing grades or chronic absenteeism.

Slide 7: Key Feature 3 - Class Committee Meetings (CCM)
• Digital Record Keeping: Move away from paper registers to a fully digital CCM portal.
• Meeting Minutes: Staff can record discussions, attendees, and key decisions.
• Student Feedback Tracking: Formally log student grievances or subject feedback.
• Actionable Follow-ups: Ensure issues raised during CCMs are assigned, tracked, and resolved systematically.

Slide 8: Key Feature 4 - Student & Staff Experience
• Student Portal: View upcoming deadlines, submit handwritten docs/code, and receive instant AI feedback. 
• Staff Portal: View unified dashboards for multiple subjects, track real-time attendance, and monitor grade distributions.
• Mark Locking & Publishing: Staff evaluate, adjust AI scores if needed, and "Publish" grades to make them visible to students.

Slide 9: Key Feature 5 - Governance & Risk Intelligence (Principal/HOD)
• Institutional Audit: Principals get a top-down view of total active subjects, staff engagement, and AI usage.
• Risk Intelligence Dashboard: Automatically identifies and categorizes "At-Risk" students based on calculated algorithms (low marks + low attendance).
• Regional Performance: HODs can compare faculty grading trends and department averages.
• Exportable Reports: Download comprehensive data as PDFs for official academic reviews.

Slide 10: Key Feature 6 - Administration & System Lifecycle
• Core System Management: The backbone of the institutional setup.
• Bulk Operations: One-click CSV imports to instantly create hundreds of Users, Subjects, and enrollments.
• Semester Transitions: Automated lifecycle management to securely archive old data and promote students to the next academic year.

Slide 11: Technology Stack & AI Architecture
• Frontend: React, Node.js, Tailwind CSS (Modern, premium interface).
• Backend & Database: Express, MongoDB (Secure, scalable data storage).
• Evaluation Engine: Python (FastAPI), Google Vision/Tesseract (OCR), Large Language Models (LLM) for Natural Language Processing.
• Hardware Requirements: Standard Server/Cloud instance (2.5GHz+, 8GB RAM).

Slide 12: Conclusion & Future Scope
• Conclusion: AAES modernizes academic excellence by combining AI-evaluation with powerful human-centric tools like Mentorship and CCM portals.
• Future Scope: Enhanced predictive student success analytics, deeper ERP integrations, and multi-collegiate regional syncing.
