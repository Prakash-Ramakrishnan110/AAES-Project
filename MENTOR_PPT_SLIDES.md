---

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**Title:** Mentorship Module
**Subtitle:** One-to-One Student Support System
**Supporting Line:** Guiding Every Student. Tracking Every Interaction.

---

## Slide 2: Why Mentorship?
**Header:** The Need for Structured Student Guidance
**Bullet Points:**
- Students face academic, personal, and career challenges that go unaddressed.
- Informal support is untracked and hard to audit.
- AAES Mentorship creates a formal, accountable support channel.
- Every mentor-mentee interaction is digitally recorded for action.

---

## Slide 3: Mentorship Ecosystem
**Header:** Three Layers of the System
**Bullet Points:**
- 🗺️ **Mapping (MentorStudentMap):** Links each student to one active mentor per academic year.
- 💬 **Queries (MentorshipQuery):** Student raises concern → Mentor responds → Resolved & logged.
- 📋 **Interactions (MentorshipInteraction):** Detailed session logs with Action Plans and Risk Levels.

---

## Slide 4: How Mentor Assignment Works
**Header:** Bulk Mentor Assignment Process
**Bullet Points:**
1. HOD or Admin selects a group of students.
2. Assigns a Staff member as their Mentor.
3. System archives the previous mentor record in History.
4. Student's profile is instantly updated with new mentor.
5. All changes are logged in the Audit Trail.

---

## Slide 5: Student Query Lifecycle
**Header:** Raising and Resolving a Query
**Bullet Points:**
1. **Student** submits a query (Academic, Attendance, Personal, General).
2. **Mentor** receives the query and reviews it.
3. **Mentor** replies and optionally sets a follow-up date.
4. Query is marked **Resolved** and locked from further changes.
5. HOD & Admin can view all queries for the department.

> 🔒 Spam Control: Students are limited to a maximum of 3 open queries at a time.

---

## Slide 6: Risk-Based Interaction Tracking
**Header:** Identifying Students at Risk
**Bullet Points:**
- Every session is logged with a **Risk Level**:
  - 🟢 **Green** – Student is on track.
  - 🟡 **Yellow** – Needs monitoring.
  - 🔴 **Red** – Requires immediate intervention.
- Principal's dashboard aggregates risk data across all departments.
- Enables proactive early intervention before academic failure.

---

## Slide 7: Key Governance Features
**Header:** Accountability & Oversight
**Bullet Points:**
- **MentorHistory:** Tracks all mentor changes over time — no data is lost.
- **Audit Logs:** Every mentor assignment is recorded with who assigned, when, and why.
- **HOD View:** Monitors mentorship coverage — ensures no student is unassigned.
- **Principal Analytics:** Sees institution-wide risk distribution.

---

## Slide 8: Impact & Benefits
**Header:** Outcomes of the Mentorship System
**Bullet Points:**
- 📈 Increases student engagement and accountability.
- 🎯 Provides actionable data to identify at-risk students early.
- 📑 Creates an audit-ready record of all student support activities.
- 🤝 Bridges the gap between students and faculty in a structured way.
